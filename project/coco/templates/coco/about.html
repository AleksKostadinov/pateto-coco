About Pateto Coco

Meet Pateto Coco, the little rubber duck with an adventurous spirit and an insatiable curiosity. Pateto Coco loves to travel and explore the world, always on the lookout for new and exciting places to discover.

Despite being small in size, Pateto Coco has a big personality and an even bigger heart. He's not afraid to take risks and try new things, and he approaches every adventure with a sense of wonder and enthusiasm that's infectious to everyone around him.

From swimming in the deep blue sea to soaring through the clouds in a hot air balloon, Pateto Coco has seen and done it all. He's faced challenges and overcome obstacles, but he's always emerged stronger and more resilient than before.
